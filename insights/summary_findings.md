## Key insights
- **Geography:** 4,119 customer cities across 27 states; several cities show no orders — expansion opportunity.  
- **Trend:** Orders grew strongly through mid‑2018; sharp anomaly/drop in Sep–Oct 2018 that requires investigation.  
- **Seasonality:** Peaks in **Nov–Jan** and **May–Aug**.  
- **Hourly pattern:** Afternoon > Night > Morning; Dawn is lowest.  
- **Top markets:** SP leads in customers, orders, and total revenue; PB has highest average order value.  
- **Revenue growth:** Payments (Jan–Aug) grew **~136.98%** from 2017 → 2018.  
- **Logistics:** Freight and delivery vary widely by state — some states show high average freight and long delivery times.  
- **Payments:** Most customers use single-installment payments; credit cards are dominant.

## Top recommendations
- **Investigate anomaly:** Confirm cause of Sep–Oct 2018 drop before forecasting.  
- **Market expansion:** Pilot campaigns in underpenetrated cities; ensure logistics readiness.  
- **Promotion timing:** Schedule promos in Afternoon/Evening; test low-cost “dawn” offers for off-peak lift.  
- **Freight optimization:** Route consolidation, carrier renegotiation, and improved fulfilment placement.  
- **Delivery KPI:** Improve ETA forecasting; track OTIF by state and prioritize slow regions.  
- **Payments strategy:** Promote low-fee digital payments and short-installment BNPL; simplify credit-card checkout.
